Celebrity

Vijay Thalapathy, commonly referred to as Thalapathy Vijay, is a renowned Indian actor and playback singer who primarily works in Tamil cinema. He was born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Vijay is one of the highest-paid actors in the Tamil film industry and enjoys a massive fan following, often being referred to as "Thalapathy," which translates to "Commander" or "Leader" in Tamil. Vijay made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Naan Sigappu Manithan" (1985) and later appeared in a few other movies. He then made his leading role debut with the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" in 1992. However, it was his role in the film "Poove Unakkaga" (1996) that brought him commercial success and established him as a bankable actor. Some of Vijay's most successful and acclaimed films include "Ghilli" (2004), "Thuppakki" (2012), "Kaththi" (2014), "Mersal" (2017), and "Bigil" (2019), among others. He is known for his charismatic screen presence, powerful dialogue delivery, and his ability to perform action, romance, and emotional scenes with ease. Apart from his acting career, Vijay has also sung numerous songs in his films and has received appreciation for his singing talent. Vijay's popularity extends beyond the borders of Tamil Nadu, with a significant fan base across India and among the Tamil diaspora worldwide.His fans often celebrate the release of his movies with grand festivities and social initiatives.
